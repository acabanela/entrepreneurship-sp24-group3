About Us

Welcome to Emotion Explorers! We are a startup emerging from the MSDS 603 Data Entrepreneurship course, committed to developing innovative solutions for individuals with neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs). Our focus is on harnessing cutting-edge technology to enhance emotional intelligence and social interaction capabilities.

Our Mission and Vision

At Emotion Explorers, our mission is to leverage Computer Vision AI to empower and enable individuals with neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs) to better understand emotions and facial expressions, creating a fun environment for social connection, confidence, and belonging.


We aim to provide accessible and effective tools that enhance emotion recognition for people with NDDs. We envision a world where these individuals can engage in social interactions more confidently and independently.

Our Technology

Our platform leverages advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to analyze facial expressions and provide interactive feedback. This enables users to improve their emotion recognition skills in a user-friendly environment, making use of real-time data processing and customized feedback loops.

Our Commitment

We commit to continuous innovation and empathy-driven design to provide scalable solutions that not only meet the current needs but also anticipate future challenges in emotion recognition training.
