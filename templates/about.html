About Us

Welcome to Emotion Explorers! We are a startup emerging from the MSDS 603 Data Entrepreneurship course, committed to developing innovative solutions for individuals with neurodevelopmental disorders (NDDs). Our focus is on harnessing cutting-edge technology to enhance emotional intelligence and social interaction capabilities.

Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide accessible and effective tools that enhance emotion recognition for people with NDDs. We envision a world where these individuals can engage in social interactions more confidently and independently.

Our Technology

Our platform leverages advanced computer vision and artificial intelligence to analyze facial expressions and provide interactive feedback. This enables users to improve their emotion recognition skills in a user-friendly environment, making use of real-time data processing and customized feedback loops.
